I'm Diana, a Portuguese photographer and filmmaker with a deep love for visual storytelling. With a degree in Digital Arts and Multimedia from ESAD, I've built a practice rooted in both technical craft and artistic curiosity, always searching for the image that says what words can't.

Basel in Porto, I work on projects locally and abroad, drawn to the kind of work that pushes creative boundaries and leaves a mark. I thrive on experimentation, adapt quickly to new environments, and bring genuine passion to every project I take on.

If you're building something that deserves to be seen, I'd love to be part of it.
