Munna:
Living Tales

Jan. 2025

Co-directing, Co-Production, Camera Operation, Editing

This project involves the production of an artistic video lasting around three minutes and six short videos focusing on the brand's products for commercial purposes. We aim to explore a visual world where the classical and the dreamlike merge into a single universe, following the journey of two characters who, upon discovering the furniture, encounter a new reality, emerging into an environment where 'classics' take on a new meaning.

The contrast between the house's classical architecture and the new approach to the furniture's characteristic style not only highlights MUNNA's craftsmanship but also emphasises the inspiration that cultural heritage offers the brand. In this way, the videos not only promote the products but also reinforce MUNNA's identity as a link between tradition and innovation in interior design.
