T1 Jams

Jul. 2025

T1 Jams was launched as an initiative created by Eduardo Hora, Guilherme Aguiar and Joana Pinto with the aim of promoting new emerging artists from Porto. More than just a concert, it is a space for sharing and expression for lovers of music and art where, in an intimate setting, the artist and the audience are part of creating a moment in which the present takes precedence over everything else.

The first edition featured performances by ManWillOnly and Lopo Romão.
